Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track

The TVL ICT Track is a specialized strand under the Senior High School Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL)program designed to equip students with essential skills and knowledge in information and communications technology. It focuses on both theoretical foundations and practical applications in areas such as computer programming, web development, hardware troubleshooting, networking, and digital communication systems.

Animation

DepEd TVL ICT Animation is a specialized course within the Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE) program that focuses on Information and Communications Technology (ICT). This course equips learners with the skills to create clean-up and in-between drawings while developing an understanding of essential competencies for a career in the animation industry. The curriculum also covers personal entrepreneurial skills, environmental and market awareness, and technical animation knowledge, preparing students for diverse career opportunities in the field.

Programming

DepEd TVL Programming is a specialized strand that equips students with essential skills in computer programming, web development, and multimedia creation. This program aims to prepare learners for future careers in the fast-growing IT and digital industries by combining hands-on practical projects with in-depth theoretical knowledge. Students will develop competencies in areas such as ASP.NET MVC applications, HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript programming, while also enhancing problem-solving, creativity, and critical thinking skills necessary for a professional environment.
