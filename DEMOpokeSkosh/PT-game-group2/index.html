Your Pokémon Journey Begins

Welcome, aspiring Pokémon Trainer! In this world, every great adventure starts with a single choice — your character. Will you take on the role of the determined boy or the courageous girl? The choice is yours to make.

Once you've chosen your trainer, you’ll step into the vibrant world of Pokémon where friendships and battles await. You'll give your trainer a name, marking the beginning of your unique journey through lush towns, wild forests, and challenging gyms.

The path ahead won't be easy — you’ll select your difficulty level, shaping the strength of your Pokémon and the challenge of your foes. Whether you prefer a relaxed stroll through the world or an intense battle to the top, there’s a difficulty that fits your style.

Next, the excitement truly begins as you enter the Pokémon selection. Twelve unique Pokémon await your choice — but you can only pick three to become your trusted companions. Each Pokémon has its own moves and personality, so choose wisely to build a team that fits your strategy.

After selecting your team, you will decide the order in which your Pokémon will battle. The battle order can be the difference between victory and defeat, so think carefully!

Prepare yourself as the screen zooms in with the iconic Pokémon logo, and the battle begins! Both you and your opponent will send out your Pokémon in thrilling turn-based combat. When a Pokémon faints, another steps up to continue the fight, keeping you on the edge of your seat.

Defeat your rivals one by one — from your friendly Rival to gym leaders like Brock, Misty, and Lt. Surge. Each victory brings you closer to becoming the ultimate Pokémon Master.

Once you've conquered all four trainers, celebrate your achievement with your loyal Pokémon by your side. You can then choose to start a new adventure or return to the home page.

Your journey is filled with strategy, courage, and friendship. Are you ready to take the first step and become a legend in the world of Pokémon? The adventure awaits!
