Welcome to Flip Frenzy!

A fun memory match game. Flip the cards to find all matching pairs!

How to Play

  • Click on two cards to reveal them.
  • If they match, they stay flipped. If not, they flip back.
  • Match all pairs within the time limit to win the game.
