Catch the Falling Fruit - Instructions

Objective

Catch the falling fruits using the basket at the bottom of the screen. Avoid missing fruits or you'll lose lives!

Controls

  • Use your mouse or arrow keys to move the basket left and right.
  • Catch fruits to gain points.
  • Missing fruits will cost you lives.

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: Slow falling speed, more lives.
  • Medium: Moderate speed and lives.
  • Hard: Faster fruits, fewer lives.
  • Extreme: Very fast falling fruits, very limited lives.
